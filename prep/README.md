# Prep Knowledge Layer

This directory is the structured landing zone for interview-prep artifacts that currently live across loose folders on the Desktop.

## Subdirectories

- `companies/`: company dossiers, research notes, and interview packets
- `topics/`: reusable topic preparation such as systems design fundamentals
- `flashcards/`: generated or curated flashcard sets
- `debriefs/`: post-interview reflections and lessons learned
